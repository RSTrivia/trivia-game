How to Play

⚔️ The Goal: Answer OSRS trivia questions to gain XP and level up your character. Unlock pets & achievements on your way!

👤 Accounts: Create an account to access Daily and Weekly Modes, earn XP, climb the leaderboards, and claim your achievements.

🛡️ Normal Mode: Build your score through a continuous streak of randomized questions; a single incorrect answer ends the game.

⚡ Lite Mode: A 100-question challenge for a quick session.

📅 Daily Mode: Play the daily challenge once per day to earn bonus xp. Tackle 10 random questions that are the same for everyone.

🏃‍♂️ Weekly Mode: Play the weekly challenge as many times as you want and improve your weekly score. Tackle 50 random questions that are the same for everyone that week.

🥇 Leaderboard: View global rankings for the highest score in all game modes and total XP earned.

🔗 Share: Share your daily challenge score with your friends!

🏆 Collections: View your pets and achievements.
